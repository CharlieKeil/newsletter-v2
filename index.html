Construction Begins!

The work has started as we received our construction permit in August. The existing sign along the County Rd. 17 was removed to leave the space for building a new uni pole structure that will go 24 ft high. The structure will be able to hold 2 10 ft x 20 ft LED digital billboard for eastbound and westbound traffic. Mojak Construction is responsible for the foundation and excavation work and Al-Dan Steel is responsible to build the metal structure.

It is exciting times for ALLEA as we are seeing a possible launch date of October 5th of a project which took 1 year to plan and secure financing.

Giving back to the community

One of the objectives of the project is to give back to the community. The unsold empty spots can be used for charities or non-profit local organizations. They must be from the municipality of Clarence-Rockland.

The organizations can contact us directly at messagecenter@alleasolutions.com
